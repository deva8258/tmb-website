Founded by engineering students, TMB is driven by the vision to digitally transform how buses are tracked and managed in India. We combine hardware and software to create a reliable transport intelligence platform.

What We Do

Track My Bus is building a centralized digital platform for real-time bus tracking across India.

We connect buses, operators, and passengers through a single intelligent system that provides accurate real-time location and reliable travel information. TMB supports all types of buses and uses its own GPS devices installed in each vehicle to transmit live data to secure cloud servers. Passengers track buses through the TMB mobile app, while operators monitor and manage their fleets using a dedicated dashboard.

"Our mission is to modernize bus transportation in India by delivering accurate tracking, better operational efficiency for operators, and a stress-free travel experience for passengers."
